## How to sign

This project uses an automated signing flow (CLA Assistant Lite). When you open your first Pull
Request, a bot will comment requesting your signature. To sign, leave a comment on the PR with the
exact text:

> **I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA**

Your signature (your GitHub username and the PR number) is recorded in the central repository
[`StrangeDaysTech/cla-registry`](https://github.com/StrangeDaysTech/cla-registry), in the `weft/`
folder, which is the verifiable source of truth for this project's signatures. The comment you leave
on your PR is the original evidence of your signature (see section 8); the central registry points to
it and freezes the version of the CLA you signed. You only need to sign once; future contributions
are covered as long as the content of this CLA does not change. If the CLA is updated to a new
version, you will be asked to sign the current version again.
